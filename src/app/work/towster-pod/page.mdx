## Overview

TOWSTER Pod is a patent-pending, dual-use, hyperbaric, warming, amphibious medevac pod. It is designed to be versatile for varied operational environments such as amphibious littoral, ground, arctic, etc. The pod is dual-purpose, serving both tactical and medical logistics during infiltration and exfiltration. It features a compact accordion-like design and is lightweight when deflated. It is adaptable for multiple manned/unmanned transport methods including underwater, aerial, and ground. The pod also transmits vital signs to the receiving platform.
