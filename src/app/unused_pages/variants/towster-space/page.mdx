## Overview

In potential collaboration with NASA and SpaceX, the TOWSTER Space Pod is a cutting-edge solution engineered to facilitate the medical evacuation of astronauts during extravehicular activities (EVAs) in space. This specialized pod is designed to ensure the safety and well-being of astronauts during critical missions beyond Earth's atmosphere.
