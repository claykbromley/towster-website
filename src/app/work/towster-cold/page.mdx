## Overview

The Towster Arctic Pod is a specialized, multi-use MEDEVAC pod specifically designed for high-altitude and extreme cold-weather environments. Ensuring reliable operation in the most severe conditions, this pod is an essential tool for varied operational environments including arctic, mountainous, and other high-altitude regions. The pod is dual-purpose, serving both tactical and medical logistics during infiltration and exfiltration.
